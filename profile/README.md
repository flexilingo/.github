# FlexiLingo

**Learn any language through content you love.**

AI-powered language learning platform that turns podcasts, videos, articles, and conversations into interactive learning experiences with CEFR-leveled subtitles, spaced repetition, and real-time AI feedback.

### What you can do

- **Watch & Listen** — Learn from podcasts, YouTube, Netflix, Spotify, and 20+ platforms with interactive subtitles
- **Review & Remember** — Spaced repetition with Leitner, SM-2, and FSRS algorithms
- **Talk & Practice** — Real-time AI voice conversations adapted to your level
- **Read & Explore** — Import articles, PDFs, and books with word-level analysis
- **Track & Grow** — XP, streaks, daily stats, and CEFR progress tracking

### Languages

`English` · `Spanish` · `French` · `German` · `Chinese` · `Arabic` · `Persian` · `Turkish` · `Hindi` · `Russian`
